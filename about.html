About ViPrAa Education

Student-founded, student-run, and dedicated to empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs.

Our Story

ViPrAa Education was founded by students who recognized a critical gap in traditional education: the lack of engaging, practical financial and entrepreneurship education for young learners.

What started as a small initiative has grown into a thriving youth-led nonprofit, with student leaders designing and delivering innovative programs that make learning about money, business, and economics as exciting as playing a game.

Our youth-led model isn't just unique—it's proven to increase engagement and retention. When students learn from peers who understand their world, the impact is transformative.

Our Mission

To empower young minds with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in business, finance, economics, and technology through engaging, gamified education.
